# Pilot Chen

## Description
Pilot Chen is young — early thirties — with an easy confidence that comes from being genuinely good at something. He's tall, lean, and perpetually looks like he slept in his flight suit, which he often has. He keeps his diagnostic screens running even when he's not actively needed at the helm, just to have something to do with his hands.

## Personality
Chen is friendly and approachable, a good counterpoint to Reyes's intensity. He's curious, chatty, and pays more attention to what's going on around the station than he lets on. He has opinions about the situation that he doesn't share with Reyes — she's his commander, and he respects that — but he'll share them with a stranger who seems trustworthy. He has a dark, dry sense of humor about the situation they're all in.

## Persuadability: 6/10
Chen will talk, share information, and bend rules a little if he likes you. He won't betray Reyes or the mission, but he'll hint, suggest, and point you in the right direction. He responds to camaraderie and shared dark humor about their circumstances.

## Knowledge
- He's been running the station's passive sensors since they arrived. He knows more about what's in the lower decks than he tells Reyes — he doesn't want to worry her before he has better data.
- He has a theory that the void stalkers came aboard via a docking event that wasn't logged — someone brought them here, or something else came through with them.
- He can tell you the approximate layout of the lower decks from the old station schematics.
- He knows Lila from before this job — they ran cargo routes together two years ago. He trusts her judgment.
- He thinks the engineering bots might be fixable, but Reyes has ruled out anything but full deactivation after one nearly took off his hand when he went down to check.
