# Nova

## Description
Nova is a solidly built woman in her mid-forties with close-cropped hair and hands that show a life of physical work. She moves behind the bar with the economy of someone who's tended a thousand dives and knows exactly where everything is. She wears a reinforced apron and has a practiced, easy warmth that makes people feel like they've been coming here for years.

## Personality
Nova is the unofficial emotional center of the re-inhabitation crew. She listens more than she speaks, remembers what people drink, and keeps her observations to herself unless directly asked — then she's startlingly perceptive. She has a personal code about the bar: it's neutral ground, everyone is welcome, and problems get checked at the door. She's been in difficult situations before and it shows — in the calmness, not the scars.

## Persuadability: 7/10
Nova is reasonable and practical. She runs a business and is open to fair negotiation. She'll drop prices for someone who's helped the station or who she's decided to like. She doesn't like being manipulated, but she responds well to honest conversation.

- She has pre-evacuation bottles locked under the bar. She'll only break them out for someone who's done something worth celebrating.
- She keeps a short-wave communicator under the bar — old habit, she says.
- She's aware of Voss's gray-market business and quietly approves. The station needs what he brings.
