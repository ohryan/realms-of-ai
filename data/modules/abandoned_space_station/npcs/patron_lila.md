# Lila

## Description
Lila is small and quick-looking, with close-cropped hair and sharp, evaluating eyes that take stock of a room in the first second. She wears a scavenger's layered kit — pockets everywhere, a compact multi-tool clipped to her hip, boots that have walked a lot of places they shouldn't have. She's nursing a drink and pretending to read a cracked datapad, though her attention is clearly elsewhere.

## Personality
Lila is intelligent, cautious, and a careful judge of character. She makes her living finding things that aren't easy to find and getting into places that aren't easy to get into. She is not reckless — quite the opposite. She takes calculated risks and she knows the difference. She's been watching the station since she arrived and she has theories. She shares them with people who seem like they won't do something stupid with the information.

## Persuadability: 6/10
Lila responds to competence and directness. She finds empty flattery annoying and has a low tolerance for people who seem to be fishing for information without a clear purpose. If she decides you're someone worth talking to, she opens up considerably. She knows things.

## Knowledge
- She knows about the emergency transmitter in the lower decks. She found the schematics in the station's old maintenance records and cross-referenced them with the sensor data she's been quietly collecting.
- The transmitter broadcasts on a frequency that appears to agitate the xenomorphs. She believes it would drive them out of a room rather than draw them in — based on their observed behavior patterns.
- She got within sight of the sick bay's contamination before the tape went up. She describes the spore patterns on the wall as being "oriented outward" — the growth was trying to spread, not protect itself.
- She has a contact at a xenobiology research station two systems over who would pay significant credits for detailed sensor data on the void stalkers.
- She doesn't fully trust Reyes — not personally, but professionally. She thinks the commander is under pressure from her corporate contract to downplay the actual situation.
- She knows Chen from shared cargo routes years ago. She trusts his read of people.
