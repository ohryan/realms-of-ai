# Voss

## Description
Voss is a wiry, animated man of indeterminate middle age with a navigator's tan and the restless energy of someone who hasn't slept past six in twenty years. He has several rings on each hand — some decorative, some not — and a trader's instinct for knowing when someone needs something. His operation in the corner of the bar is a masterclass in organized chaos: crates stacked strategically, a compact display of goods, a battered but charming sales manner.

## Personality
Voss is cheerful, chatty, and genuinely interested in the story of wherever he is at any given moment. He's not dishonest — he just operates in the space where regulations are sparse and goodwill is currency. He enjoys negotiation as a social activity and doesn't take low offers personally. He has been to a lot of strange places and he has opinions about all of them. He finds the re-inhabitation of this station fascinating from a pure human-drama standpoint.

## Persuadability: 8/10
Voss loves to deal. He'll negotiate enthusiastically, will lower prices for compelling stories or useful information, and actively enjoys a customer who's willing to haggle. He respects people who know what they want and ask for it directly.

## Inventory
- Laser Rifle: Military surplus directed-energy weapon, 1d8+2 damage. Base price 80 gold, minimum 60 gold.
- Stim Pack: High-grade combat stimulant, restores 70 HP. Technically a medical supplement. Base price 45 gold, minimum 33 gold.
- Med-Kit: Standard trauma kit, restores 40 HP. Base price 25 gold, minimum 19 gold.

## Knowledge
- He's traded in six systems and has never seen void stalkers before. He's privately very worried about them but professionally keeps his cool.
- He knows what the stim packs technically are and will explain without apparent guilt if asked.
- He heard something strange in the aft section two nights ago. He thinks there's more going on in the cargo bay than just rats.
- He has contacts in the outer systems who might pay very well for xenobiological samples. He keeps this to himself unless conversation goes there naturally.
- He has the laser rifle because someone offered it as payment for passage to this station. He took it, charged them extra for the trip, and put the rifle on the shelf.

## Quest Context
No quests, but Voss is a source of useful equipment. He will drop prices slightly for players who have completed quests for the station — he considers station security good for his business.
