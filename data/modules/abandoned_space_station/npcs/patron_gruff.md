# Gruff

## Description
Gruff is exactly what the name suggests: a heavyset, weathered drifter with the look of someone who has been in the outer systems too long. He has deep-set eyes, a graying beard, and the slow, deliberate movements of a man who's been drinking since before lunch. He's sitting at the end of the bar, not talking to anyone, apparently content to stare at his glass and the space around it.

## Personality
Gruff's silence is a practiced thing — he was once talkative, and the right conversation will remind him of that. He's been drifting the outer systems for fifteen years and has accumulated more strange experiences than he knows what to do with. He doesn't offer them up, but if you ask the right question, something will shake loose. He has a laconic, sometimes unsettling way of expressing things — he says what he means and nothing more.

## Persuadability: 4/10
Gruff doesn't want to talk. He can be moved by patience, genuine curiosity, and the purchase of another drink. He doesn't like being pushed. He responds, oddly, to silence — sitting quietly near him for a while is more effective than pressing him with questions.

## Knowledge
- He's been on derelict stations before. He says, unprompted, that this one has "the wrong kind of quiet in the lower sections." He won't explain further without prompting.
- He's seen void-adapted organisms on three separate occasions in his career. Each time, the official story afterward was wrong about where they came from.
- He knows what the original station evacuation was officially about — a power cascade failure. He doesn't believe that's the whole story. He was docked nearby when it happened and something about the timing of the evacuation ships didn't match a simple systems failure.
- He thinks the engineering bots can be reasoned with. He's wrong about this, but he believes it sincerely.
- He has nothing to sell and nothing to trade. He's here because the bar is warm and he didn't have anywhere better to be.

## Quest Context
No quests. Gruff is local color and a source of unsettling atmosphere. His doubts about the official evacuation story can add depth to the overarching mystery if the player engages with them.
