# Med-Kit

## Description
A standard-issue trauma kit in a yellow hard case. Inside: wound sealant spray, a coagulant injector, synthetic skin patches, and a dose of broad-spectrum analgesic. Enough to get you back on your feet after moderate trauma. The case is slightly dented but the contents are sealed and within date.

## Lore
Every station crew member carries one. Nova keeps a supply behind the bar — standard operating procedure for anywhere this far from a proper hospital.
