# Stim Pack

## Description
A high-pressure auto-injector loaded with a combat stimulant cocktail — clotting agents, adrenaline analogue, and a fast-acting regenerative compound. Press the flat end against any exposed skin and it fires. The effect is immediate and overwhelming: pain suppression, accelerated tissue repair, heightened alertness. The crash afterward is not pleasant, but that's a tomorrow problem.

## Lore
Not technically legal in regulated space. Voss sells them as "emergency medical supplements" and doesn't look you in the eye when he does. They work. That's what matters out here.
