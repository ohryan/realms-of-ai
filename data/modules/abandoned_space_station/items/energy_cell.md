# Energy Cell

## Description
A compact power cell, the kind used to charge personal equipment. In a pinch, a field-modified cell can be cracked open and the biogalvanic fluid inside applied to wounds — it has mild antiseptic and cell-stimulating properties. It's about as medical as a car battery, but it's better than nothing.

## Lore
Nova rigs these herself — the field-mod trick is old spacer knowledge. It's either this or drink yourself to a lower pain threshold, and the cells are cheaper.
