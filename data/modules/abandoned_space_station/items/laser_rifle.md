# Laser Rifle

## Description
A long-barreled directed-energy weapon, military surplus but well-maintained. The focusing lens is clear and undamaged. A charge indicator along the barrel reads full. It's heavier than the pistol but the beam it fires is considerably more persuasive.

## Lore
Voss acquired this from an Outer Coalition decommission auction — at least, that's what he'll tell you. The stock has been refinished and the barrel is a replacement. Someone went to a lot of trouble to keep this rifle operational.
