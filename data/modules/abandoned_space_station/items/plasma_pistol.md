# Plasma Pistol

## Description
A compact sidearm with a grip worn smooth from use. The plasma chamber glows faintly blue when charged. It fits the hand well — clearly someone's personal weapon, not standard issue. The sights are scratched but accurate.

## Lore
The standard backup sidearm for independent contractors working the outer systems. Not military-grade, but reliable. This one has a few dents and a serial number filed off, which tells a story of its own.
