# Void Rat

## Description
A lean, hairless rat the size of a housecat, adapted over generations to life aboard spacecraft. Its skin is pale and mottled, eyes entirely white and milky — perfectly suited for navigating dark corridors. Its teeth have been worn to sharp points on metal and polymer. It looks wrong in the way that things look wrong when they've evolved somewhere they shouldn't have.

## Combat Behavior
Fights in a pack mentality even when alone — darting in, biting hard, retreating to circle. It aims for the ankles and wrists, trying to disable rather than kill outright. Doesn't know fear. Will fight to the death over its nest territory.

## Combat Flavor
- It lunges low and fast, teeth snapping at your ankle before you can react.
- A blur of pale flesh — it bites down on your wrist and wrenches sideways.
- It squeals and charges, moving in an unpredictable zigzag pattern.
- It feints left, then darts right, claws raking across your shin.

## Lore
Void rats are a known problem on long-haul stations and derelict vessels. They breed fast, eat anything, and adapt to microgravity within a generation. Pest control is a standard line item in any station's operating budget. This station's budget ran out three years ago.
