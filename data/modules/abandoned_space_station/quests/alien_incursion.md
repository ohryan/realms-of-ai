# Alien Incursion

## Description
Commander Reyes is asking you to go into the lower decks and eliminate the void stalkers nesting there. This is the final obstacle standing between the station and full operational status.

## Background
The void stalkers appeared in the lower decks approximately six months after the original evacuation — according to the station's automated sensor logs, which kept running on emergency power. How they arrived is unknown. They are not native to this region of space. They are not in any xenobiological registry. They have had three years to establish a nest in the crew quarters and secondary storage sections of the lower decks, and in that time they have grown territorial, numerous, and organized under an alpha. No member of the re-inhabitation crew has successfully entered the lower decks. Two attempts were made in the first week. Neither attempt reached the bottom of the maintenance ladder. Reyes has been waiting for the right opportunity and the right person. The station cannot be declared operational while the lower decks are unpassable — critical systems in those sections need to come back online. She knows this is the most dangerous thing she's asking of anyone.

## Narrative Notes
This is the climactic quest. The lower decks should feel genuinely threatening — the environment is transformed, the enemies are hard, and the alpha is a real test of everything the player has built up. The mood is one of deep unease and earned danger. Completion represents the full restoration of the station. Reyes will be — for the first time in the module — overtly, personally grateful. She's a reserved person and this will show in the cracks. The station is saved. The mission is accomplished. That matters.
