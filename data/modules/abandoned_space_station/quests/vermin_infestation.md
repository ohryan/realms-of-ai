# Vermin Infestation

## Description
Nova, the bartender, has asked you to clear the void rats from the cargo bay before they make their way into the bar's supply stores.

## Background
The cargo bay has been infested with void rats since before the re-inhabitation crew arrived. They've been breeding in the freight container stacks for years, growing larger and more aggressive on a diet of insulation, polymer packaging, and whatever organic material they could find in the station's recycling systems. Nova has twice found evidence of rats in the aft corridor — close enough to her supply stores to worry her. She's asked you to go down there and deal with them. She's offering gold from the bar's discretionary fund. "The crew's morale depends on this bar staying operational," she told you. "That means my supply stores stay rat-free."

## Narrative Notes
This quest is the station's entry-level problem — a practical, grounded task that establishes the player as someone capable and willing to help. The rats are aggressive and not trivial, but this is fundamentally a pest control job. Nova is grateful and shows it. The completion should feel like a small but real victory — the station is incrementally safer and better off. Nova's warmth upon completion is genuine. She cares about the station succeeding.
