# System Reboot

## Description
Commander Reyes has tasked you with destroying the corrupted security bots in the engineering deck so that power distribution to the lower levels can be safely restored.

## Background
When the station was evacuated three years ago, the security bots in engineering were left on an indefinite patrol loop with a standing order not to stand down until a command reset was issued. The command reset was never sent. The bots have been running the same patrol, slowly degrading, ever since. Their programming has deteriorated to the point that they treat any human presence as a hostile threat with maximum response authorization. Reyes sent a crew member down to attempt a voice override on day two of the re-inhabitation. He came back with a baton burn across his forearm and a strong opinion about the matter. Physical deactivation is the only option. The bots need to be cleared before Reyes will authorize restoring power to the lower levels — too much risk of a power surge event with active combat units in the engineering stack.

## Narrative Notes
This quest raises the stakes from pest control to genuine danger. The bots are a harder fight and represent the station's past — systems that kept running faithfully after everyone left, now turned hostile through nobody's particular fault. The mood should be slightly melancholy alongside the danger. Reyes is grateful upon completion in her characteristic understated way — she might simply say "good work" and mean more by it than the words suggest. The completion unlocks access to the lower decks section of the station's recovery.
