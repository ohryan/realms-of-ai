# Medical Corridor

## Description
A sterile-looking corridor connecting the main passage to the sick bay. The walls are a pale clinical white, though grime and dark stains have settled into the corners. Biohazard warning tape has been stretched across the entrance to the sick bay to the east. Warning placards read: CONTAMINATION RISK — DO NOT ENTER.

## Atmosphere
The corridor feels colder than the rest of the station. Something about the scrubbers in this section over-compensates, keeping the air dry and sharp. The biohazard tape sways slightly in the circulation draft. Nobody on the re-inhabitation crew has been willing to go past this point.

## Notable Features
- Biohazard warning tape stretched across the sick bay entrance
- CONTAMINATION RISK warning placards on the east wall
- A defibrillator unit mounted in a broken emergency case
- Smeared handprints on the wall heading toward sick bay — old, dark

## Secrets
The handprints are not blood. Analysis would show it's a spore-based biological residue — the same substance the mycelid growth produces. Whatever made them was trying to get out, not in.

## Sounds and Smells
An over-active air circulation system making the corridor feel like a wind tunnel. A faint organic smell drifts from the east — something earthy and wrong, like soil left to rot in a sealed room.
