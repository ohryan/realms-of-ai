# Engineering Deck

## Description
Engineering is the mechanical gut of the station — a dense forest of reactor columns, coolant pipes, and power distribution arrays. Caution-striped panels line every wall. Two of the station's security bots are here, their optical sensors burning a hostile red. Their programming has degraded into something between guard behavior and predatory instinct. They will not let you pass unchallenged.

## Atmosphere
The engineering deck was never meant to be a comfortable place, and it isn't. It's hot, loud with the thrum of reclaimed power systems, and riddled with blind corners. The security bots were locked into a patrol loop when the station was evacuated and left running on emergency power. Three years of looping the same route with no input has not improved their judgment. A maintenance ladder descends further to the lower decks.

## Notable Features
- Reactor columns and coolant pipe runs throughout the chamber
- Power distribution arrays with active warning lights
- Two security bot charging cradles — both empty
- A maintenance ladder descending through a sealed grate to the lower decks
- A wall-mounted engineering terminal, still partially functional
- Emergency pressure suits hanging near the ladder — standard issue, worn

## Secrets
The engineering terminal, if accessed, shows the bots' last command log. Three years ago, the final human entry reads: "Override alpha-seven — patrol mode indefinite. Do not stand down until command reset." Nobody ever sent the reset command.

## Sounds and Smells
A constant deep mechanical thrum. The whir and click of bot servos on patrol. Heat radiating from the reactor columns. The smell of ozone, hot metal, and hydraulic fluid.
