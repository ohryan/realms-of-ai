# Docking Bay

## Description
The docking bay is a cavernous space, still smelling of ozone and old exhaust. Scorch marks streak the deck plating where ships once sat. Emergency lighting casts everything in a sickly amber. Ahead, a sealed bulkhead door leads north into the station's main corridor.

## Atmosphere
The station's power has only recently been restored. Half the overhead lights flicker. The bay feels vast and empty — a place built for dozens of ships that now holds none. Boot prints in the grime suggest the re-inhabitation crew came this way not long ago.

## Notable Features
- Scorch marks on the deck where docking clamps once held ships
- A manual crank panel to open the bulkhead north — now running on restored power
- Maintenance lockers bolted to the east wall, most stripped bare
- Emergency lighting strips along the floor, amber and stuttering

## Secrets
A close inspection of the maintenance lockers reveals one still has its panel screwed shut from the inside. Something scratched at it from within, long ago.

## Sounds and Smells
The hiss of atmosphere recyclers kicking back to life. Stale air with an undertone of burnt polymer. Distant clanking from somewhere deeper in the station.
