# Cargo Bay

## Description
The cargo bay is a vast, shadowed chamber stacked with sealed freight containers, some dating back to the station's original occupation. Emergency lighting barely reaches the far walls. Something has nested here. Gnawed wiring, shredded packing material, and droppings litter the floor. The void rats are everywhere — lean, hairless, adapted to zero-g and recycled air, and extremely aggressive.

## Atmosphere
The cargo bay feels like a different world from the clean ambitions of the bridge. It's where the station's failure is most visible: expensive cargo left to sit for years, some containers breached and looted before the evacuation, others still sealed — contents unknown. The rats have made a labyrinth of the container stacks. There's a maintenance hatch in the floor leading down to engineering.

## Notable Features
- Rows of sealed freight containers, some decades old
- Gnawed wiring and chewed packing material throughout
- Rat nests built into the gaps between containers
- A maintenance hatch in the floor leading down to engineering
- Several containers with their seals broken — one contains what appear to be personal effects

## Secrets
One of the oldest containers, marked with a corporate logo that no longer exists, contains a deactivated combat drone. It's not one of the station's security models. Nobody knows who shipped it here or why.

## Sounds and Smells
Scrabbling, clicking claws on metal. High-pitched squealing from deeper in the stacks. The acrid smell of rat musk and chewed polymer insulation.
