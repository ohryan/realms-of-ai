# Bridge

## Description
The command bridge occupies the forward crown of the station, a horseshoe of cracked display screens and worn control consoles. Through the panoramic viewport, stars hang motionless in the black. Commander Reyes stands at the central command station, face lit by the blue glow of a restored navigation panel. Pilot Chen is at the helm, running diagnostic sequences.

## Atmosphere
The bridge feels like the beating heart of the re-inhabitation effort. Equipment cases are stacked to one side, still half-unpacked. Someone has pinned a hand-drawn station map to the wall beside the main console. The crew here are focused and professional, but there's an undercurrent of tension — they know better than most what the station's deeper sections still hold.

## Notable Features
- Panoramic viewport with an unobstructed view of deep space
- Central command console with restored navigation displays
- A hand-drawn station map pinned to the bulkhead
- Equipment cases from the re-inhabitation supply drop, still being unpacked
- A holo-projector showing a 3D layout of the station with several zones marked in red

## Secrets
The holo-projector's red zones correspond to areas the crew has deemed too dangerous to enter yet. The lower decks are marked with a skull icon and the annotation: "STAY OUT — unknown life sign signatures."

## Sounds and Smells
The soft chime of diagnostic routines completing. The low thrum of main power running through the console. Clean, recycled air — the bridge air scrubbers were the first thing restored.
