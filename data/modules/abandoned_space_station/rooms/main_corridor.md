# Main Corridor

## Description
A wide pressurized corridor forms the spine of the station's habitation ring. Overhead signage — faded but readable — points to the bridge to the north, the medical wing to the east, and the bar to the west. A maintenance ladder descends toward the aft section below. The docking bay lies south.

## Atmosphere
This corridor was the artery of daily life when the station was fully staffed. Now it's half-lit and eerily quiet. The re-inhabitation crew has taped temporary power conduits along the walls. Cables snake across the floor where they haven't finished the proper routing. It has the smell of a place waking up from a long sleep.

## Notable Features
- Overhead signage with directional arrows to key station sections
- Temporary power conduits taped to the walls
- A cracked viewport on the east wall showing the star field outside
- Maintenance ladder running down through a grate in the floor to the aft corridor

## Secrets
One of the overhead signs has been hand-modified — someone scratched out the original text under "Cargo Bay" and wrote "DON'T" in capitals.

## Sounds and Smells
The hum of reclaimed power systems. Distant voices from the direction of the bridge. A faint burnt-metal smell from the temporary wiring.
