# Short Sword

## Description
A reliable blade, well-balanced for quick strikes. The steel is clean and the edge maintained. Someone took care of this one.

## Lore
Gerald calls it "Aldenmoor steel" with the enthusiasm of a man who bought it in bulk. It's not fancy — no engravings, no name, no history worth telling. But it's solid, it's balanced, and it won't shatter when you need it most.

A meaningful step up from a dagger. Worth the coin if you're planning to go somewhere dark.
