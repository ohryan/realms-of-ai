# A Rat Problem

## Description
Old Mira has asked you to clear the rats from the tavern cellar.

## Background
The rats appeared about three weeks ago. At first just one or two — Mira thought little of it. Then the gnawing started at night, and she found one of her grain sacks torn open. By the time she understood what she was dealing with, they'd multiplied.

She can't go down there herself. Her hip. And she can't afford to lose more supplies. The cellar holds everything she needs to keep the tavern running through the rest of the wet season.

She's embarrassed to ask for help. She's more embarrassed about how bad it's gotten.

## Narrative Notes
This quest is simple on its face but carries weight for Mira. She's not just paying for pest control — she's paying to keep the tavern alive. She put everything she had into this place.

The reward (50 gold) is nearly more than she can afford. She'll pay it because she made the offer, but she feels it.

If the player negotiates hard, Mira will resist — not because she's cheap, but because she's proud. She'll only yield if the player makes a genuinely good argument, and even then she'll feel it.

After the quest is complete, Mira should feel genuine relief, not just businesslike satisfaction. She might let something slip about how much this place means to her.
