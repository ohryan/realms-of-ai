# Giant Rat

## Description
A bloated rat the size of a cat, with yellowed teeth and a mangy, patchy coat.

## Combat Behavior
Fights with desperate, frantic energy. It doesn't retreat. When wounded, it becomes more erratic — lunging and snapping without coordination. It goes for the ankles and hands.

## Combat Flavor
Possible attack descriptions (the narrator or future AI combat system can draw on these):
- It lunges low, teeth snapping at your ankle.
- It rears up, claws raking the air before finding flesh.
- A frenzied bite that draws blood before it scrambles back.
- It squeals and rushes forward, all aggression and no technique.

## Lore
These aren't ordinary rats — they're unnaturally large, possibly fed on something they shouldn't have found in the cellar. Mira doesn't know what, or she won't say.

There's a gap in the north cellar wall. That's where they came from. Where they came from before that is another question.
