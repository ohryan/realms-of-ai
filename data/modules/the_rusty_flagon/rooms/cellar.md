# Tavern Cellar

## Description
A damp, musty cellar filled with barrels of ale and rotting sacks of grain. Water drips from the stone ceiling. Something has been gnawing through the supplies.

## Atmosphere
The air is cold and smells of wet stone, mold, and something animal. The light from above barely reaches the far corners. Barrels are stacked in unsteady columns. Several sacks of grain have been torn open — the contents scattered and fouled.

There's a low, intermittent scratching sound. Claws on stone. It's close.

## Notable Features
- Rows of ale barrels, some tapped, most sealed and labeled in Mira's cramped handwriting
- Torn grain sacks with scattered, fouled grain — at least a week's supply ruined
- Droppings along the base of the east wall, a clear trail
- A broken lantern on the floor near the stairs, oil long since spilled and dried
- Scratch marks on the wood of two barrels, deep enough to be claws
- A gap in the north wall where the stone has crumbled — roughly the size of a large cat

## Secrets
The gap in the north wall connects to a narrow tunnel that runs under the alley behind the tavern. This is how the rats got in. The narrator can mention the cold draft coming from that direction, but not explicitly say "this is where they came from" unless directly asked.

## Sounds and Smells
Dripping water. The smell of mold and animal musk. The sound of skittering that stops when you move. A faint, sour odor from the fouled grain.
