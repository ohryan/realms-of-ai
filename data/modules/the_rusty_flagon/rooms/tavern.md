# The Rusty Flagon

## Description
A dimly lit tavern thick with the smell of cheap ale and pipe smoke. Hunting trophies line the smoke-stained walls. A fire crackles in the hearth, casting long shadows across the sawdust floor.

## Atmosphere
The place has seen better days. The wooden beams are warped and dark with grease. Candles burn low in iron holders crusted with old wax. The fire gives the room a false warmth — comfortable from a distance, suffocating up close.

The sound of rain can sometimes be heard beyond the shuttered windows. The north road is flooded. Nobody's coming or going for a day or two.

## Notable Features
- A mounted boar's head above the bar, one glass eye cracked and cloudy
- A trapdoor behind the bar leading down to the cellar
- A noticeboard near the door with curling, water-damaged notices
- A locked strongbox beneath the bar (Mira keeps the key)
- Gerald's trading goods piled in the corner: crates, wrapped bundles, a merchant's scale
- A dark stain on the floorboards near the hearth that no amount of sawdust quite covers

## Secrets
If pressed, the narrator can hint that the stain near the hearth is old blood — there was a fight here years ago, before Mira's time. She doesn't know the story. The noticeboard has a faded notice about a missing person from two seasons ago, name illegible.

## Sounds and Smells
Cheap tallow candles. Smoke. Wet wool. The creak of the sign outside in the wind. Occasionally the skitter of something small behind the walls.
